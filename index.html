I'm a graphic / web designer currently based in Verona, NY. I studied graphic design at Mohawk Valley Community College before transferring to SUNY Cobleskill.

My time in college not only furthered my interest in different forms of design but also introduced me to web design and development which has become a huge interest of mine since. I'm always trying to develop new skills and techniques in all areas in both of these areas of interest.
